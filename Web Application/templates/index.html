About This Tool

This app uses artificial intelligence to analyze chest X-ray images for signs of pneumonia. The AI model has been trained on thousands of X-ray images to detect patterns associated with pneumonia.

How to Use:

  1. Upload a clear chest X-ray image (JPG or PNG format)
  2. Click "Analyze Image" to process the X-ray
  3. View the results indicating either "Normal" or "Pneumonia detected"
